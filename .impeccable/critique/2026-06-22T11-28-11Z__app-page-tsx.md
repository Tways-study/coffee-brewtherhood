## Anti-Patterns Verdict

**Does this look AI-generated? No.** Navy ground is real (not the cream/sand default), no gradient text, no eyebrows, no fake hero metrics, no identical card grids, no side-stripe borders. Real local copy (Jaro, Plus code, named reviewers, actual peso prices) and a real Google Maps embed all read as genuinely built for this business, not templated.

**Deterministic scan**: `detect.mjs --json app` -> exit 0, zero findings. Clean.

**Browser evidence**: No browser-injection overlay tool was available in this environment, so the official `detect.js` overlay flow could not run. As a substitute, the live site was driven directly with Playwright: no console errors, no broken images, no horizontal overflow at 1440px or 390px. But a full-page capture without an actual incremental scroll showed Story, DrinkGallery, Reviews, and Visit rendering as completely blank navy voids -- their `whileInView` reveal animations never fired. Scrolling the page incrementally (mimicking a real visitor) fixed it instantly; every section painted correctly. This is a real instance of a banned pattern: content gated behind a scroll-triggered reveal with no visible-by-default fallback. Invisible to a normal human scrolling at normal speed, but risky for link-preview scrapers, some screen-reader virtual-scroll modes, print/PDF export, or unlucky timing on a slow device.

## Overall Impression

A confident, real cafe site, not a template -- the navy/pink discipline and real content carry it. The gap is mostly about finishing rather than redesigning: the reviews carousel can't be paused, the menu has inconsistent pricing, and the scroll-reveal pattern has no safety net if it doesn't fire.

## What's Working

- Color discipline: pink stays reserved for the two primary CTAs and review highlights, never decorative.
- Real, specific local detail throughout (named reviewers, actual peso prices, real Plus code, real map pin) gives authenticity no template could fake.
- The Story section's pull-quote beat gives the scroll a real emotional turn instead of stacking interchangeable sections.

## Priority Issues

- **[P0] Testimonial carousel can't be stopped or read at the visitor's own pace** -- `components/ui/testimonials-carousel.tsx` runs an infinite scroll with no pause-on-hover (hover only scales the individual card; the track keeps moving underneath) and no manual controls.
  Fix: pause the track's animation on hover/focus, or add prev/next controls.
  Suggested command: /impeccable harden

- **[P1] Reveal animations ship sections blank if the scroll-reveal never fires** -- confirmed via browser evidence: Story, DrinkGallery, Reviews, and Visit render as empty navy boxes under non-incremental-scroll rendering.
  Fix: default these sections to visible, with the whileInView transform as enhancement only.
  Suggested command: /impeccable animate

- **[P1] Menu pricing is inconsistent** -- only 2 of 7 menuHighlights items (Liwanag, Alab) carry a price note; the other five have none.
  Fix: either price all seven or remove price from all seven for consistency.
  Suggested command: /impeccable clarify

- **[P2] Reviews carousel avatars are identical grey-silhouette placeholders** sitting next to real names and real review metadata.
  Fix: consider removing avatars entirely rather than a placeholder that contradicts the real-content brief.
  Suggested command: /impeccable distill

- **[P2] "View the menu" CTA links to the photo gallery, not an actual menu** -- sets an expectation the gallery doesn't deliver.
  Fix: rename the CTA to match what it actually shows.
  Suggested command: /impeccable clarify
